So Much More Than A Machine Shop
Since 1918, we at Murray Latta Progressive Machine have established ourselves as leaders of custom industrial machinery manufacturing, parts & equipment distribution, and millwrighting & machinery moving

CUSTOM MACHINERY
MANUFACTURING

We operate in a full-service, fully-equipped 60,000 sq ft manufacturing facility. Services offered include design & detailing, CNC & conventional machining, metal fabrication & welding, and equipment modification, overhaul & rebuild. [READ MORE]

Equipment & Parts
